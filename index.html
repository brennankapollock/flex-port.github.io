About Me

after years pursuing a career in academia and the church as an episcopal priest i realized that path wasn't for me. while my studies in philosophy and theology still excite me i needed another direction to place my intellectual yearnings. that's how i reconnected with my love of tech, gaming, and computer science. i get to collaborate and craft products with others similar to how an architect and their carpenters construct a house from the ground up. the possibilities are seemingly endless and that's enough to put me behind the keys everyday.


looking to the future i plan to learn C# and Python alongside network architecture to lend my hand in the field of cybersecurity and machine-learning.


I aim to provide front-end design and implementation services to such a team, as well as back-end API and architecture implementation. I'm experienced in a core set of front-end technologies (HTML/CSS/JS, React, Bootstrap), and I'm continuing to develop my understanding of core back-end technologies (Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL).


when i'm not writing algorithms or tinkering with hardware i love to write poetry. i was nashville's featured poet last spring and am currently working on a surrealist-fiction short story. you can find my poetry at the link below.
